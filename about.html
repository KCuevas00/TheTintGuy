OUR STORY

BUILT ON

The Tint Guy Corporation was built around one idea: give every vehicle the finish and features it deserves, done right the first time. From window tint to full custom builds, we treat every job — big or small — with the same level of care.

We're a full-service shop. That means tint for your daily driver, a sound system that actually hits, a starlight headliner that turns heads, ambient lighting, rock lights, wraps, and mechanic work — all handled personally.

No middlemen, no outsourcing. Every appointment is booked and managed directly, so you're always talking to the person actually doing the work.
