MY STORY

HOW I
STARTED

I started out doing tint on my own car because I couldn't find a shop nearby that did it the way I wanted. A few friends asked me to do theirs next. Then their friends did. A few years later that turned into full builds, audio, lighting, wraps, and headliners

Today, I handle window tints, custom sound systems, interior lighting, wraps, and general mechanic work, often more than one of those in the same appointment.

There's no front desk and nobody else touching your car. You book through a DM, I confirm a time, and I'll be the one doing the work when you show up.
